About Oggy

Oggy is the main protagonist and one of the titular characters of the series who is "the constant victim of the cockroaches".

Oggy's characteristics

  1. He's an optimistic and fun-loving kitten.
  2. He communicates with sound effects of a cat, rather than being voiced.
  3. He is a kitten with a light blue body, black dotted eyes, red nose, white stomach and feet.
